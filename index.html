About Me

Hello, I'm Declan Cullen, a passionate game designer and developer based in the UK. My journey into the world of game design began at South Essex College and further deepened during my studies at the University of Ipswich. Over the years, I’ve cultivated a strong foundation in creating interactive entertainment that combines narrative depth with engaging mechanics.

I recently graduated from Falmouth University with distinction, focusing on technical design. My studies centered around areas such as boids, procedural generation, L-systems, and pooling techniques. Additionally, I explored learning games, culminating in my final project, an immersive IVR training experience. My approach to game development emphasizes merging storytelling with innovative systems, resulting in projects that push the boundaries of conventional game experiences while maintaining accessibility and player engagement.
