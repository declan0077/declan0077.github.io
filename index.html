About Me

Hello, I'm Declan Cullen, a passionate game designer and developer based in the UK. My journey into game design began at South Essex College and deepened at the University of Ipswich. I recently graduated from Falmouth University with distinction, focusing on technical design.

My work blends storytelling with innovative systems, as seen in projects like my immersive IVR training experience. I’m driven by a curiosity for how games can educate, entertain, and inspire, pushing boundaries while keeping experiences accessible and engaging.

Areas of Expertise:

  • Game Systems Design & Implementation
  • Virtual Reality (VR) Development
  • Narrative Design & World Building
  • Educational and Serious Game Design
  • Procedural Generation Systems
